The Faculty of Computers and Information at Beni-Suef University is one of the specialized faculties that focuses on computer science, technology, and information systems. Its main goal is to prepare students to work in different fields of technology such as programming, data analysis, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and software development.


Main Departments:

  • Computer Science (CS): Focuses on programming, algorithms, data structures, and artificial intelligence.

  • Information Systems (IS): Focuses on connecting technology with business, system analysis, and design.

  • Information Technology (IT): Focuses on technical infrastructure, networking, and system administration.

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): A newer department that focuses on machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.
